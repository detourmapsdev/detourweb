Know more about Detour Maps

Who is Detour Community Maps?

Detour Community MAPs (a Marketing and Advertising Platform) is a division of Entire Marketing Group LLC established in 2007. The division was created to help the development of different communities in Illinois. We strive on connecting resident with local businesses and organizations to better the community by creating a LOCAL EVOLUTION campaign. This campaign encourages residents to shop local versus shopping at a corporate or chain store. By evolving the way we advertise, utilizing multimedia outlets, we strive toward evolving communities and local businesses. Resources suggest that for each dollar you spend at an independent business in your community, three or more times goes back into the local economy compared to a dollar spent at retailer owned by a chain. This means better school, security, resources, and an overall better community.

How do we connect residents to their communities?

We use different resources, both print and online, to introduce small businesses and organizations to their local residents. We do this by creating a collective advertising platform that displays local business advertisements as well as useful information, such as emergency phone numbers, local events, entertainment, and local Savings. How did we do this? We have created a printed map that pin points locations of different local businesses, restaurants, retail shops, and even public assets. With this printed map, you can navigate through your community with ease and explore different areas your probably never knew were there! A Detour Community map is a Marketing and Advertising Platform (MAP) that ARTvertises your community and makes exploring fun, colorful and easy.
This concept became so popular and useful throughout the community, that we created another platform, www.detourmaps.com . Our online directory is available 24/7 so you will have access to it from the comfort of your own home. With this online tool you can avoid traveling outside your neighborhood to see what is there. This saves you save energy, money, and time. We renovated our printed map, which is accessible to everyone, even if you do not live in the community.

Printed Map Users

What does a printed map contain?

Detour’s printed map is a way to have everything there is to know about the community in the palm of your hand. With 2 publications each year for each community and over 40,000 maps distributed you will be well informed with our printed maps. Inside the printed maps include a map of the community with different local business advertisements. The map pin points each business giving the user a better idea of where the business or resource is located in the community. We do not just have business locations on the map; we also have locations of parks, bus routes, schools, useful phone numbers, events and basically all public assets in the community. The printed map also is color coded so that residents may know which business is participating in the $10 Savings Card promotion.

How do I find a local business? ex: a restaurant

To find a place, first select the community you would like to explore. You can enter a community by clicking the community on the map or you can also look in the top navigation where it says Select your community and choose from the list of communities.
Once you are in the community you want, you can search a business by their name in the search field on the left side of the screen. If you want to narrow down your search, you can use the orange sub navigation under the main navigation and choose from the categories listed. Once you click on a category, a list will appear on the left side of the screen. Once you find the business you are looking for click on the name and it will take you to the business online bubble.

I couldn't find the place I was searching for. What can I do?

If you can't find what you're looking for, try these:
  • Make sure you've spelled the name of the business correctly for the best results.
  • Make sure you are in the correct community. Businesses are separated by community.
If you have tried all of the above, unfortunately, the businesses is not registered with us yet.

What other information can I find on detourmaps.com about each community?

Aside from business information, you can find fact and events from each community on the navigation tabs. Select your community, and go to the Navigation tab. Here, you will find tabs like Discover, Deals, and Events. In the Discover tab, there is a slide show containing information of the community, such as population, schools, and useful numbers. Each community is different. On the Deals tab, you can find which businesses are participating in the $10 savings card promotion. On that tab, you can click the business and it will direct you to that businesses bubble information. Each community also has an Event tab. The event tab will inform you of upcoming events. Click on the event to be directed to the location of the event along with the event information. Events can also be found on a business bubble by clicking Show Events (events vary for each business and not all businesses have events)

Deals

What is the $10 Savings Card and where can I get one?

With our color coded Savings Card, you can save $10 on your next purchase (minimum purchase required of $30, $40, or $50). Our Free Printed maps have savings cards attached to be redeemed at participating businesses. You can also fill out an online form to receive a digital card. This form is located on the home page and on the deals page.

The savings card is available throughout participating neighborhoods, from supermarkets and restaurants, to public locations, including libraries. Saving cards are free of cost. They are included in out printed maps, or you can download one online.

How do I know who is participating in the Savings Cards?

Using your Detour printed map, the participating businesses are color coded, depending on the minimum purchase required ($30 is yellow, $40 is red, $50 is blue).

Using detourmaps.com you can select the community you want to explore, and click on the Deals tab to find a list of participating retailers (also color coded).

How can I use the $10 Savings card?

Once you've located a deal, simply fill out the back of your card for it to be valid. And that's it! Present it at the time of purchase to save $10 off your bill. If you requested a savings card online, just fill out the information asked and print. You are ready to use your savings card.

How can I get the business’ direct coupons?

If you would like to see more features, information, and coupons of a business, you need to have an account with detourmaps.com. When you click on a feature, such as a video or review, a pop up window will give you the option to either create an account with us, or to sign in to your existing account.
